about our Project

ArtRevive, developed by CodeGeas, a team of six passionate AI students, is dedicated to preserving and revitalizing historical artworks through the power of artificial intelligence.

We combine cutting-edge technology with deep cultural appreciation to restore damaged pieces, classify authentic and AI-generated art, and raise awareness about heritage conservation. With a focus on Tunisian and global art, our mission is to protect humanity’s artistic legacy while inspiring future generations.
